OSM History Map Tag Queries

This page identifies entries in the OSM database that are tagged as historic, but without a civilization tag. The query that produces this page can be seen at BrewMap.cfg if you are interested in suggesting improvements!

The 'Browse' links take you to the OSM page for the object.

Note that the edit links do not work at the moment. I do not know why - they use the same syntax as you get on the OSM 'browse' page for the element.
